Swim suits

  • They are cool. It's hard not love them
  • Can be ordered during suit fittings
  • Not required though
  • However, please don't wear other teams' gear
  • Schedule has dates of suit fitting

Practice

  • Swimmers to have suit, goggles, cap on 5 minutes before practice start
  • Stay for entire practice session for that age group
  • Come to at least x practice sessions per week
  • Stay on deck during practice
  • Our coaches are awesome and experienced. Please give them space during practice

Meets

  • Event signup requests for swimmers can be done here
  • Needs to be done well before the meet
  • Double check the signups before the meet here
  • Sign up to volunteer for meets here
  • Cheer all swimmers - this team, other teams, first to wall, last to wall, random swimmer
  • Check in with chaperones
    before leaving
  • Have fun and let the swimmers have fun

Event assignments

  • Swimmers are assigned to events based on the priorities and discretion of the coaches
  • Parents, volunteers, parent reps can make requests
  • Coaches are likely to take that among a range of other factors into consideration
  • Bottomline: it's the coaches call
